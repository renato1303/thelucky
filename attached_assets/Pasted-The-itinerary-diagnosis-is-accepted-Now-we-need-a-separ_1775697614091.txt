PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS

The home must support at least these content types:

1. HERO CTA CARD
A top editorial/hero card that stimulates automatic itinerary creation.
Example intent:
- “Faça seu roteiro automático”
- “Monte sua viagem em segundos”
- click goes directly to the itinerary creation journey

2. EDITORIAL HIGHLIGHTS / NEWS-LIKE CARDS
Cards that feel like travel stories or highlights.
Examples:
- “O hotel que mudou o jeito de viver Barcelona”
- “3 lugares que valem a viagem só pelo pôr do sol”
- “O restaurante favorito da Carolina Dieckmann”

These must feel like curated editorial entries, not generic cards.

3. SOCIAL / FRIEND ROUTE CARDS
Cards featuring people who have route/guide content.
Examples:
- Carolina Dieckmann
- photo of the person OR
- a place they recommended OR
- a quote from them
I want freedom to choose which image/text/card style appears.

4. PLACE / ROUTE / CTA MIX
The home should allow mixing different card types in one editorial feed:
- person
- place
- route
- CTA
- quote/editorial
Like a curated Netflix-style rail or portal front page.

5. MANUAL CURATION CONTROL
I want to choose exactly what appears in the highlights.
This must NOT depend only on automatic ranking.
It should be possible to choose:
- title
- subtitle
- image
- quote
- target link
- content type
- active/inactive
- order
